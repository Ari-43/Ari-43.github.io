Ari43's Resourcepacks

My resourcepacks. Both listed here require OptiFine and are simple utility packs.

Fullbright

Description:
Makes everything appear at the highest brightness value (15). This does not affect the actual light level, just what you see.

Download:

1.15+
1.7 - 1.14.4

Mob Proofing Assist

Description:
Places where mobs can spawn are highlighted in red. Setting smooth lighting to off is recommended.

Download:

1.15 - 1.17.1 (Red: Light Level ≤ 7)
1.7 - 1.14.4 (Red: Light Level ≤ 7)
